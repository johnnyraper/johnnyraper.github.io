Process summary

I'm a multidisciplinary product designer, and work end-to-end spanning some/all of the stages of UX, UI and frontend development. I analyse competitors to identify industry trends and understand the positive and negative areas of existing products. Often I find customers have a set idea of what they require, so I work with relevant stakeholders to ascertain initial requirements, elicit these into acceptance criterion with estimations for each task. I begin by generating low-fidelity wireframes, user flows, mockups and prototypes, and we're underway.

However, if requirements are either lacking, ambiguous or unclear, I will work with stakeholders (usually the customer/team, developers, and a Product Owner(s)) to further understand the problem, and generate the required wireframes, mockups, etc. in accordance with their needs.

Once feedback is generated and iterated on, I make high-fidelity wireframes leading to the creation of UI designs. I ensure ideas are always communicated clearly to all parties, leading to the build and delivery of a functional, intuitive and beautiful product.
