Profile

With a background in computing visualisation, 2D and 3D graphics, I'm a seasoned and dynamic experience designer with an eagle eye for detail. I blend a palette of strong design skills with clean, semantic code to create memorable encounters.

Spanning well over a decade's experience, I've worked with clients such as: Microsoft, Knight Frank, The Premier League and Richard E. Grant. I'm reliable and efficient, and I bring innovative technological skills to creative teams. My tools of the trade include Figma, Sketch and Photoshop as well as VS Code for when I craft away in the frontend.

I've helped many successful brands create beautiful solutions for their customers, and have spent the last four of 12 years producing compelling user interfaces in the field of engineering – to that I remain consciously interested.
