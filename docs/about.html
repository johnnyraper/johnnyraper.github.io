Process summary

I'm a multidisciplinary product designer, and work end-to-end spanning some/all of the stages of UX, UI and on occasion, frontend development. I analyse competitors to identify industry trends and understand the positive and negative areas of existing products. Often I find customers have a set idea of what they require, so I work with relevant stakeholders to ascertain initial requirements, elicit these into acceptance criterion with estimations for each task. I begin by generating low-fidelity wireframes, user flows, mockups and prototypes, and we're underway.

However, if requirements are either lacking, ambiguous or unclear, I will work with stakeholders (usually the customer/team, developers, and a Product Owner(s)) to further understand the problem, and generate the required wireframes, mockups, etc. in accordance with their needs. Through undertaking design iterations, I'm able to turn ambiguous requirements into intuitive solutions, which delights users and exceeds expectations.

I ensure ideas are always communicated clearly to all parties, leading to the build and delivery of a functional, intuitive and beautiful product.
